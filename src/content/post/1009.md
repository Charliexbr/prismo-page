**I still don't know what I really want to do.**

I struggle with the fact that I don’t have any hobbies or strong interests. I feel unmotivated to discover new passions, and it frustrates me. The pressure of this productivity culture is overwhelming, and I’m tired of waking up each day feeling like I’m squandering my life because I’m not achieving something “amazing” or “inspirational.” It seems like we’re conditioned to believe that if we aren’t doing something extraordinary, we’re somehow less valuable. Ugh, I wish I could express this better.
