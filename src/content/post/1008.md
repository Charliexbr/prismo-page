<br />

It's hard to imagine all these messed-up folks like me who hate themselves but still crave love from others. I thought I loved you, but let's be real—it was just laziness. I used our so-called love to distract myself from my deep self-loathing and emptiness. Did it work? For a while, sure. But here I am, still drowning in my own life, and guess what? You’re drowning too. Doubts start to flood in: Do you really love me? Do we even love each other? Or are we just two sad sacks comforting each other to catch a breath in this sea of misery? I don’t know. All I know is I hate myself. How can you love me if I can’t stand being me? The version of me you love is the one I can’t even look at in the mirror. And how can I love you when I don’t even know what love is? 

It’s just so sad to see that we both can’t be who we’re meant to be. It’s a shame to realize we’ll never have the kind of relationship we always wanted.
